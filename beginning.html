THE BEGINNING

Hey, and in this webpage I am going to talk about my background life.

My time in Kuwait

I was born in Mumbai,India in 0ctober 6, 1999, I didn't spend a whole lot time in india, because when I was about 2 years old, we moved to the middle eastern country, called Kuwait. We spend a total of 7 years in that country, not only did I enjoy my experience over there, the infrastructure, food, tourism was amazing. There were also so many friends to play with,and to socialize, I will never forget their faces, since the memories will be in my head for a long time.

Getting along with a new country

After 7 years enjoying ,In 2008, we came to the United States since my dad got a green card, well when I was new to the US I did not really understand their slang terms, and their english was really hard to understand, and because of this I did'nt really enjoy so much over here, I also forced my parents to let's go back to Kuwait. I was feeling very lonely, since I did not have anyone to socialize with. As the years went along, I was eventually satisfied with the place, even though it took a lot of time to get over it. Now I live in Atlanta, GA, and hope to have more fun with my friends and relatives.
